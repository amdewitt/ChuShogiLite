Historic Chu Shogi Puzzles

This is a collection of 200 historic Chu Shogi Puzzles, which is a subset of 224 puzzles from the 1992 Middle Shogi Manual (MSM) by George Hodges. The SFENs were provided by H. G. Muller, and the solutions and repairs were verified with HaChu, Muller's Chu Shogi engine.

The puzzles are tsume shogi problems, which are forced mate problems where the attacker must constantly check the enemy King. They are split into 4 series of 50 problems each (not counting different versions of the same problem) according to how they were in the original source material, with one exception. 24 of the problems in the MSM's original D-series were left out due to being difficult to repair or impossible. The first 50 of the remaining problems were renamed to account for this, and the excess problems were moved to the A-series (A25-A50).
