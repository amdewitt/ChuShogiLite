Historic Chu Shogi Puzzles

This is a collection of 250 Chu Shogi Puzzles (not counting different versions of the same puzzle), powered by ChuShogiLite. The first 224 of these (Series A, B, C, and D) are a collection of 224 puzzles from the 1992 Middle Shogi Manual (MSM) by George Hodges, and the SFENs were provided by H. G. Muller. The rest were sourced from the author and the TumeChu Collection. All solutions and repairs were verified with HaChu, Muller's Chu Shogi engine.

The puzzles are tsume shogis, which are forced mate puzzles where the attacker must constantly check the enemy King. The first 224 of these are split into 4 series according to how they were in the original source material, and the rest were placed in a separate series.

You can download the puzzle SFENs, the author's notes, and PNG diagrams of the puzzles in both Japanese and Mnemonic piece fonts by clicking here.
